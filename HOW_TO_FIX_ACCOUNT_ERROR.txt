┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  OPTION 1: Get Real Bakong Merchant Account (RECOMMENDED)          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Step 1: Download Bakong App
   📱 iOS: App Store → Search "Bakong"
   📱 Android: Play Store → Search "Bakong"

Step 2: Register as Merchant
   • Open Bakong app
   • Choose "Register as Merchant/Business"
   • Complete registration form
   • Submit required documents (Business license, ID, etc.)

Step 3: Get Approved
   • Wait for KYC verification (1-3 business days)
   • Receive your merchant account ID

Step 4: Update Your Code
   • Open: .env file
   • Change BAKONG_ID="sopheanan_khem@aclb"
   • To:    BAKONG_ID="your_real_merchant_id@aba"
   • Change BAKONG_MERCHANT_NAME to your business name

Step 5: Clear Cache
   Run: php artisan config:clear

Step 6: Test
   ✅ Scan QR code → It works!

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  OPTION 3: Test Without Real Scanning (FOR DEVELOPMENT)            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

For testing your app WITHOUT a real Bakong account:

1. Go through checkout normally
2. Reach the payment page with QR code
3. Note the MD5 hash (visible in URL or page)
4. Open: http://127.0.0.1:8000/khqr-simulator
5. Enter the MD5 hash
6. Click "Simulate Payment"
7. ✅ Order completes successfully!

This lets you test the entire payment flow without scanning.
